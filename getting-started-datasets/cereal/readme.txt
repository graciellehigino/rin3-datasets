Description: ~1990s Nutrition data on 80 cereal products from the USA


Data Set Fields:

- name: Name of cereal

- mfr: Manufacturer of cereal
American Home Food Products
General Mills
Kelloggs
Nabisco
Post
Quaker Oats
Ralston Purina

- type: hot or cold cereal product
cold
hot

- calories: calories per serving

- protein: grams of protein per serving

- fat: grams of fat per serving

- sodium: milligrams of sodium per serving

- fiber: grams of dietary fiber per serving

- carbo: grams of complex carbohydrates per serving

- sugars: grams of sugars per serving

- potass: milligrams of potassium per serving

- vitamins: vitamins and minerals - 0, 25, or 100, indicating the typical percentage of FDA recommended vitamins and minerals per serving

- shelf: display shelf (1, 2, or 3, counting from the floor)

- weight: weight in ounces of one serving

- cups: number of cups in one serving


Acknowledgements: These datasets have been gathered and cleaned up by Petra Isenberg, Pierre Dragicevic and Yvonne Jansen. The original source can be found here: https://lib.stat.cmu.edu/datasets/1993.expo/ with full description of the use case.
