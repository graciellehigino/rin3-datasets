Description: Bike sharing counts aggregated on daily basis for 731 days in 2011 and 2012 from Capital Bikeshare system, Washington D.C., USA which is
publicly available in http://capitalbikeshare.com/system-data. Weather information are extracted from http://www.freemeteo.com.



bikeshare data set fields


- index: record index (an ID number)

- season: season of the year when the bikes were rented

- year: year when the bikes were rented

- month: month when the bikes were rented with 1 = January and 12 = December

- holiday: whether the day was a holiday or not according to the District of Columbia
Department of Human Resources holiday schedule (https://dchr.dc.gov/page/holiday-schedules)

- weekday: day of the week when the bike was rented

- workingday: whether the day was a working day or not (nonworking days could be weekends or holidays)

- weather: what the weather was like on the day the bike was rented

- casual_users: number of casual users that rented bikes

- registered_users: number of registered bikeshare system users that rented bikes

- total_users: total number of users that rented bikes
